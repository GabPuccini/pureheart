Common questions

Good to know.

What is PureHeart?

PureHeart is a local charity organisation. We run five multi purpose halls available for hire and three active fundraising campaigns that support the local community.

Can anyone hire a hall?

Yes. Our halls are available to individuals, families, community groups and businesses. We host weddings, classes, workshops, meetings, parties and community events.

How are donations used?

Every pound donated goes directly to one of our three active campaigns: Warm Winters, Food at the Heart, or Open Doors. Donors choose which cause to support at checkout.

Is PureHeart a registered charity?

Yes, PureHeart is a registered UK charity. Our charity number is shown in the footer of every page.

Can I volunteer with PureHeart?

Yes. We welcome volunteers across all three campaigns and at events held in our halls. Get in touch through the contact page and we will match you to a role that suits your time and interests.
