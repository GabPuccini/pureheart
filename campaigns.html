Donor FAQs

The honest answers.

Where does my donation go?

100 percent of your donation goes to the campaign you choose. PureHeart covers payment processing fees from hall hire income, so the full amount reaches the cause.

Can I claim Gift Aid?

Yes. If you are a UK taxpayer, ticking the Gift Aid box adds 25 pence to every pound at no cost to you.

Can I set up a monthly donation?

Yes. Choose the monthly option on the donation form and we will send you a setup link by email.

Will I get a receipt?

Yes. A receipt is emailed automatically after payment. Annual summaries for tax purposes are sent each April.

What if my donation is over £1,000?

Larger donations get a personal call from a trustee within a day, and an invitation to come and see your impact at the halls. Email hello@pureheart.example to arrange.
