Booking FAQs

Quick answers.

How do I book a hall?

Submit the booking form on this page with your preferred hall, date and time. We confirm availability within one working day, normally faster.

How far in advance can I book?

Up to twelve months in advance. We recommend booking at least four weeks ahead for weekend events.

Is a deposit required?

A refundable deposit of £100 secures your booking. The balance is payable seven days before your event.

Can I bring my own catering?

Yes. All halls allow external catering. The Kitchen Hall and Oak Hall have full catering kitchens you can use.

Are the halls step free?

Yes. All five halls are step free at the entrance. The Oak Hall and Garden Room have accessible toilets on the same level.
